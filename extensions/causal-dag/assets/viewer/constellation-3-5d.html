What you're looking at
A causal DAG of an agent session, drawn against first-occurrence order. The central axis runs from earliest to latest; radial distance shows tree depth and angle identifies a branch. Solid edges are the canonical backbone, node color carries status, and dashed arrows are annotation links.
How to interact
